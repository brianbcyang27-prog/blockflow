Welcome to BlockFlow

A simple system to balance your day — focus, rest, and everything in between.

  • 📋 Set 3 daily blocks: Focus, Personal, Recovery
  • ⏱️ Use the timer to work distraction-free
  • 🚫 Track distractions to build awareness
  • 📅 Plan events with AI in the Calendar tab
📋

Your 3 Daily Blocks

Each day is split into 3 blocks. Set a time for each, work through them, and mark them done.

  • 🎯 Focus Block — Work, study, deep tasks
  • 📚 Personal Block — Hobbies, projects, learning
  • 🧘 Recovery Block — Rest, social, unwind
⏱️

Timer & Auto-Break

Use the timer to work in focused sprints. When it ends, auto-break gives you 5 minutes to rest.

  • ▶️ Press Start Focus to begin
  • ⏸️ Pause any time — focus time is saved
  • ☕ Auto-break (toggle in Settings)
  • 🔔 Sound notifications (toggle in Settings)
🚫

Build Better Habits

Tap "I got distracted" when you catch yourself scrolling. Track sleep, check your history, and plan events with AI in the Calendar tab.

  • 📊 Check 7-day history to see your progress
  • 📅 Plan events with AI in Calendar
  • ⚙️ Customize everything in Settings
