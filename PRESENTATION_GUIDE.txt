# BlockFlow v4.0 Presentation Guide
====================================

## Opening (30 seconds)
"Today I'm excited to show you BlockFlow v4.0 — a major update that transforms our productivity tool into an AI-powered personal assistant. We've added 6 new features that make planning your day effortless."

---

## Feature 1: Daily Briefing (1 minute)
**What to say:**
"Every morning, BlockFlow now generates a personalized briefing. It looks at your calendar, history, and goals to suggest how to plan your day — including energy management tips."

**Demo steps:**
1. Point to the "Daily Briefing" card on the dashboard
2. Show the morning summary with suggested blocks
3. Highlight the energy planning feature

**Key point:** "This saves you 5-10 minutes every morning planning your day."

---

## Feature 2: Productivity Heatmap (1 minute)
**What to say:**
"The new heatmap gives you a visual overview of your week. You can instantly see where you spent your time — focus work, personal activities, or recovery."

**Demo steps:**
1. Scroll to the heatmap card
2. Point out the color coding (blue=focus, green=personal, amber=recovery)
3. Show how it visualizes your last 7 days

**Key point:** "At a glance, you know if you had a balanced week."

---

## Feature 3: AI Weekly Report (1 minute)
**What to say:**
"At the end of each week, you can generate a comprehensive report with one click. It includes statistics, insights, and recommendations — and you can download it as a PDF."

**Demo steps:**
1. Click "Generate Report" button
2. Show the generated insights
3. Mention the PDF download option

**Key point:** "No more manual reporting — AI does it for you."

---

## Feature 4: Quick Capture (1 minute)
**What to say:**
"Sometimes you just need to jot something down quickly. The Quick Capture button is always available — just click the lightning bolt or press Cmd+K."

**Demo steps:**
1. Click the ⚡ floating button
2. Type a quick task
3. Show it saves instantly

**Key point:** "Capture ideas in 2 seconds without leaving what you're doing."

---

## Feature 5: Natural Language Scheduling (1 minute)
**What to say:**
"This is my favorite feature. Just type what you want in plain English — like 'Team standup tomorrow at 9am' — and AI creates the calendar event for you."

**Demo steps:**
1. Type a natural language event
2. Click Parse
3. Show the AI-analyzed preview
4. Click Confirm

**Key point:** "No more filling out forms — just say what you need."

---

## Feature 6: Demo Mode (30 seconds)
**What to say:**
"For presentations like this, we have a Demo Mode that instantly loads sample data so you can see all features in action."

**Demo steps:**
1. Click "Load Demo Data" if not already loaded
2. Show the populated dashboard, calendar, and materials

**Key point:** "Everything you see is real functionality — just with sample data."

---

## Closing (30 seconds)
"To summarize: BlockFlow v4.0 adds Daily Briefing, Productivity Heatmap, AI Weekly Report, Quick Capture, Natural Language Scheduling, and Demo Mode. Plus we fixed bugs and added full iPhone 7 support. Thank you!"

---

## Quick Reference Card
Keep this handy during the presentation:

1. Daily Briefing → Dashboard, top card
2. Heatmap → Dashboard, below briefing
3. Weekly Report → Dashboard, generate button
4. Quick Capture → ⚡ floating button (or Cmd+K)
5. NL Scheduling → Dashboard, text input
6. Demo Mode → Settings page button

---

## Backup Answers

A: "We use NVIDIA's LLM API for natural language processing and event analysis."

Q: Is this production-ready?
A: "Yes — all features are fully functional with error handling and responsive design."

Q: Does it work offline?

Q: What about security?
A: "Firebase authentication with optional cloud sync. Data stays local by default."
